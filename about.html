History of Davinco

The company was founded under the name KC Concentrates in 1990. The original company only focused on producing concentrates for wine. In 2018 the company was bought by the current owner, Henrik Nielsen, who renamed the company Davinco.
Under the new ownership the company has expanded and inflicted a new department, in which they focuse on the production of broth, glace and sauce to cantines and public institutions.
Today Davinco works as a production company which main focus is to produce products and make private labels for other companies.
