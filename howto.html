How to make your wine in 5 easy steps

  • Home

Ingredients

Guide

You get a plastic jerrycan, with 250 ml of grape concentrate.
Then you add 4-5 l of warm water.

After that you add the yeast, that will start turning the sugar into alcohol.

Then when the mixture stops bubbling you add yeast stop.
This will make sure the sugar conversion won't keep going.

Lastly you're going to add the clearing, this will make the wine clearer and more pleasant to look at.
This will also make residue collect at the bottom of the can.
